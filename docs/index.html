Species You May Recognize

  • American Bullfrog

    The American Bullfrog, scientific name Lithobates catesbeianus is the largest of all American frogs!

  • Red-Eyed Tree Frog

    Red-Eyed Tree Frogs, scientific name Agalychnis callidryas, have special footpads that help them stick to the canopy of their rainforest home!

  • Poison Dart Frog

    Poison Dart Frogs, scientific name Dendrobatidae, come in a variety of bright colors that all say one thing- "I'm toxic! Don't eat me!"
